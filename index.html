Information and Facts about parrots

A third of the world parrots face extinction, loved by some and hunted by many, parrots taste with the tops of their beaks. there are around 393 species of parrots,parrots eat with their feets , most parrots can imitate sound, parrots can live for over 60 years, they mate for life, a baby parrots are called a chick, they are one of the most intelligent birds, parrots lay between 2 and 8 eggs at a times.
